# Agri-Data-Analysis
Strategic data analysis and market intelligence samples within the Agribusiness and Rural Development sectors.
Agricultural Strategic Market Analysis & Data Operations
Executive Overview
This project demonstrates advanced data-driven methodologies used to optimize agricultural operations and market entry strategies in Myanmar. Leveraging over 22 years of expertise, these samples reflect strategic insights developed for multi-million dollar initiatives.

Key Highlights & Expertise
Market Intelligence & Operations: Orchestrated strategic planning for a $18.5M Fertilizer project at Marubeni, focusing on market share expansion and supply chain optimization.

Data-Driven Decision Making: Analyzed complex datasets to provide actionable insights for international organizations, including ADB and LIFT-UNOPS.

AI & Digital Transformation: Integrated AI Data Operations and Digital Tools to modernize rural development tracking and food security assessments.

Project Categories
Market Analysis: Quantitative assessment of the fertilizer and agrochemical sectors in Myanmar.

Social Impact Data: Statistical analysis of food security determinants in rural areas (e.g., Paletwa case study).

Strategic Documentation: High-level reporting and business communication frameworks used in international trade and consultancy.
